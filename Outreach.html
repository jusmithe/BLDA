One of the most important ways that BLDA serves the community is by sending instructors to local schools to bring ballroom and latin dance opportunities to southside students.

BLDA instructors teach dance in after-school enrichment programs and offer all-day workshops in high school and middle school Spanish classrooms. We always welcome new opportunities to share dance with local students, and we are excited to be forming relationships with schools throughout the southside community.

If you are a teacher or administrator interested in arranging a workshop or class through BLDA, or if you are a student with dance expereiince interestein joing our team of volunteer instructors, please contact us at bldawebandmoney [at] uchicago [dot] edu.
