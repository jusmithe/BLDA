Florists Sponsors Chicago Ballroom

Serenata Flowers is proud to sponsor the Chicago Ballroom & Latin Dance Association. The Chicago Ballroom Dance Team embodies the pure spirit and joy of dance. Their success at offering an outlet for young adults to celebrate and embrace their individuality is to be applauded. Dancing is an expression, a celebration and to those of the Chicago Dance Team, just pure fun! We at Serenata believe that the gift of dance that this group delivers, goes hand in hand with our philosophy on the gift of flowers. Most often the giver gets as much joy as the recipient in the heartfelt thanks, sparkling eyes and warm smiles that always accompanies a gift of flowers.

SerenataFlowers.com is the UK's leading online flower delivery company. Founded in 2003 and the floral provider to the BBC, well-known celebrities and many, many more, SerenataFlowers.com is a direct-to-the-consumer florist, bypassing the chains of high street flower shops and middlemen, by importing flowers direct from the best growers and auctions around the world. If you wish to send flowers to Chicago, Serenata offers low unbeatable prices and fresh award winning flowers.
