Due to the great success of our programs and the interest of our students in Salsa and Ballroom Dancing, we have created two new stable dancing bodies: the Salsa Performance Group and the Ballroom Team . Our Goals are:

  • Offer a venue for advanced instruction for our students.
  • Offer the possibility to showcase our students skills in various settings. Both groups showcase choreographies at various events organized by the BLDA and other RSOs, while the Salsa Performance Group also specifically dances at the two Chicago Salsa Congressos and the Ballroom team competes at various competitions at other universities.

If you are interested in joining us, or want to have us perform at your event, please email:

  • Salsa Performance Group: bldawebandmoney [at] uchicago [dot] edu
  • Ballroom Team: ballroomteam [at] uchicago [dot] edu

Salsa Performance Group

The Salsa Performance Group is a great opportunity to learn new and more advanced moves, perfect and polish the known ones, and showcase your talents to your friends and fellow students. The goal is to learn and prepare performances to be shown at the Chicago Salsa Congresses but also in events such as OLAS, ManosUnidas, Dance Marathon, where people can learn more about our club and join us in the fun of dance!

We usualy have two rounds of open auditions--at the beginning of the school year in early October and once again in March. Everyone is welcome to come to try-outs! Performers will then practice 1 hour a week, with more practices scheduled before performances.

So come try us out! If you have any questions about auditions, the group or if you want to schedule a performance please email us at bldawebandmoney [at] uchicago.

Ballroom Competition Team

The University of Chicago Competitive Ballroom and Latin Dance Team was founded in the Fall of 2007 to provide talented students the opportunity to compete at ballroom dance competitions at the intercollegiate level. The team currently has around 10 active members. Besides competing, the team also perform in campus events like the OLAS show, Dance Marathon, etc.

More information coming soon, but email ballroomteam [at] uchicago [dot] edu.
