About BLDA

The Ballroom and Latin Dance Association is run as a University of Chicago Registered Student Organization. We teach weekly lessons each academic quarter (three levels of salsa, plus one or two special classes like chacha, mambo, or bachata) and we also organize monthly DJed dances at Seven Ten Lanes, 1055 E 55th St, as well as the occasional special dance or workshops.

Our goal is to promote the love of Latin dance on campus and in the neighborhood, and to provide the University of Chicago/Hyde Park community with affordable dance opportunities, so our lessons are $4-$5 and our dances are always free.
