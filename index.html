Welcome to our website! Currently, we update our facebook page much more often. You can find it here. We are an RSO at the University of Chicago dedicated to promoting the awareness and appreciation of ballroom and latin dances on campus, as well as providing affordable, weekly dance classes in anything from Salsa to Waltz.

Announcements:

Spring Sequences Starting!
March 30 or 31

Though the exact date is unknown (administration is still getting back to us on when Ida Noyes will be available to us), we're finally (after a whole week!) getting our spring quarter sequences up and running!
This quarter we will be changing things up a bit. Instead of just two levels of classes, we will now have three (for both salsa and ballroom): Beginner, Continuing, and Intermediate. We're also starting a brand-spankin-new Salsa Performance class. What does all this mean you ask? Go to our Lessons page for more information on how these work. Gah, so many new things! Get excited!

Southside Salsa at The Promontory
Wednesday, April 15, 7:00pm-12:00am
The Promontory (5311 S Lake Park Ave West, Chicago IL 60615)
NOTE: this is an 18 and older event.

Every third Wednesday of the month, join us and DJ El Caobo for a spicy night of salsa on the house! Yep: there's no cover charge, and BLDA instructors will be giving a complementary salsa lesson at 8:00pm. There will also be dance performances from professional latin dance studios throughout the night. Come sal-see and be sal-seen. For more info, check El Caobo's website and the facebook event page.
