The Ballroom and Latin Dance Association is a student organization at the University of Chicago.

We teach weekly lessons each academic quarter and organize monthly dance socials. Our lessons are $4-$5 and our dance socials are always free.

Tuesday Lessons

Spring Lessons

This Spring Quarter we will have three class levels for both salsa and ballroom: Beginner, Continuing, and Intermediate.

For those of you who took the lower level lessons last quarter, you will progress to the Continuing group. For those of you who are familiar with the two original levels of lessons taught in previous quarters, these two levels will translate to Beginning and Continuing in the new system.

We are also starting a brand-spankin-new Salsa Performance class. Gah, so many new things! Get excited!
