BLDA

We are a student-run organization dedicated to promoting the appreciation of ballroom and latin dances. We provide affordable, weekly dance classes spanning anything from Salsa to Waltz.

Learn more

Weekly Tuesday Lessons

Weekly lessons Tuesday evenings in Ida Noyes Third Floor Theater. $5 per class, $20 for each TEN WEEK ballroom or salsa sequence.

For more detailed information, check out our Lessons page!

Our instructors also offer private lessons. Contact bldawebandmoney [at] uchicago [dot] edu for more information.

Spring Sequences Starting!

This Spring Quarter we will be changing things up a bit. Instead of just two levels of classes, we will now have three (for both salsa and ballroom): Beginner, Continuing, and Intermediate. We're also starting a brand-spankin-new Salsa Performance class. What does all this mean you ask? Go to our Lessons page for more information on how these work. Gah, so many new things! Get excited!
