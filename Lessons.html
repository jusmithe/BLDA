Starting this spring quarter we will have three levels of salsa and ballroom dance lessons: Beginning, Continuing, and Intermediate. For those of you who took the lower level lessons last quarter, you will progress to the Continuing group. For those of you who are familiar with the two original levels of lessons taught in previous quarters, these two levels will translate to Beginning and Continuing in the new system.
